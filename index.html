MAXFIT

MaxFit is an extensive training class aimed to help your body work at maximum load from the start to the end of your workout to gain the best results faster.

SPINFIT

The members of SpinFit use the stationary bike which imitates the real world bicycle obstacle course to help you burn more than 500 calories per class.

POWERFIT

PowerFit is a high-strength training class that uses barbells/cardio to get maximum results. Please consult our managers and your therapist before signing up for this program.

YOGA

Yoga is perfect for everyone regardless of their fitness level. Start yoga classes and improve your balance, strength, and flexibility. Start or end your day with a yoga class!

AQUATIC PROGRAMS

We have 2 spacious swimming pools and offer a variety of aquatic classes and programs. You can sign up for a special program or simply have fun in the pools.

CAMPFIT

Have fun while mixing your interval training, body weight exercises, and strength training! This basic program is suitable literally for everyone, whatever your fitness goals are.
